About

Why identity, and why now

I've always been drawn to how large organizations run on invisible systems — the technology stack behind the scenes. Studying Enterprise Technology Integration with a cybersecurity minor, and then interning on Deloitte's Risk & Financial Advisory Cyber team, showed me the piece I actually care about most: who gets access to what, and how you prove it's really them. That's Identity & Access Management.

I'm now building deep, hands-on IAM and Azure AD skills through structured certification work and a public portfolio, while applying everything I already know about enterprise systems, governance, and clear communication. Every lab below is real work in my own Azure AD tenant — not just a certificate.
